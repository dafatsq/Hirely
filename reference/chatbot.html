Frequently Asked Questions

How to create a profile?
To create a profile, click on "Create Account" in the navigation bar, then fill in your personal information, education, work experience, and skills. Make sure to upload a professional photo and complete all required fields.
How do I apply for a job?
Browse jobs from the Jobs page, click on a job listing to view details, and click the "Apply Now" button. You'll need to submit your resume and answer any screening questions from the employer.
Can I edit my application after submitting?
Once submitted, applications cannot be edited. However, you can withdraw your application and reapply with updated information if the job posting is still active.
How long does it take to get a response?
Response times vary by employer. Typically, you should hear back within 2-4 weeks. You can track your application status in your dashboard and receive notifications when there are updates.
What should I include in my resume?
A good resume should include: contact information, professional summary, work experience (with achievements), education, relevant skills, and certifications. Keep it concise (1-2 pages) and tailor it to each job application.
